Automated Timetable Scheduling

As part of a hybrid class scheduling challenge involving 229 students, I optimized the transition from online to offline classes by efficiently generating a timetable and allocating students to in-person classes. The model was designed with key constraints: minimizing the number of times students needed to travel to attend classes on a given day whenever possible, and that every student had an equal opportunity to attend in-person sessions over time. The solution efficiently balanced these requirements, producing fair and practical group allocations.
